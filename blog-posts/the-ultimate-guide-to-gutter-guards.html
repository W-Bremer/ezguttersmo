January 24, 2025

Why Springfield Homeowners Are Ditching DIY for Micro-Mesh Guards
Clogged gutters aren’t just annoying—they’re a threat to your home’s foundation. In Springfield’s leafy neighborhoods like Rountree and Galloway, pine needles and oak blossoms turn gutters into ticking time bombs.

The Problem with Cheap Solutions

  • Plastic Screens: Warp under Missouri’s summer heat (95°F+), letting debris slip through.
  • Foam Inserts: Trap moisture, accelerating rust and ice dams.
  • Reverse Curve: Fails in heavy Ozarks rain, causing overflow.

Why Micro-Mesh Guards Win

  • 0.2mm Holes: Block pine needles, shingle grit, and even maple seeds.
  • Sloped Design: Rain washes debris off, zero maintenance needed.
  • Tested Locally: Installed on 500+ Springfield homes since 2020—0 clogs reported.

Cost Breakdown

  • DIY Big-Box Guards: $1.50/ft (last 1–2 years).
  • EZ Gutters’ Micro-Mesh: $3.50/ft (15-year warranty, free installation).
  • ROI: Save $1,200+ in 5 years on cleaning and repairs.
